Max Health MH [HP][HP] :

Body Strength BS [1][1] :

= the scale in which the damage done to an object increases

BS=1+15×bdBS=1+15×bd

for spike :

BSS=75+15×bdBSS=75+15×bd

Durability D [S][S] :

= the combination of max health and body damage in one formula so it's easier to compare it to a bullet/trap/drone

D=14×MH×BSD=14×MH×BS

MHMH : max health
BSBS : body strength

The 3 laws of Collision Mechanics CM:

  1. Every object deals a specific and constant amount of damage per loop.

  2. If the HP of an object is lower then the DPL (DPL'') of the opponent the corresping DPL of it is proportional to the ratio of its health and the opponent's DPL (DPL'').

    GH≤DPL′′→DPL∼GHDPL′′GH≤DPL″→DPL∼GHDPL″

  3. Every collision can only lasts a natural number of loops.

    fundamental equation: GHL=GH′′×DPL′′DPLGHL=GH″×DPL″DPL

    general health loss per loop
