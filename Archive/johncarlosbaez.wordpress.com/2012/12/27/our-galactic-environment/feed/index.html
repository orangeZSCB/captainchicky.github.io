Right; you can get to the nearest star as quick as you like, from your point of view… but if you come back you’ll see that a fair amount of time has gone by here on Earth. So if we could build fast spaceships we could explore the Galaxy in very little time—from the viewpoint of the explorers. But to send a message from one star to one at the other end of the Galaxy and get a reply would take 200,000 years, so our civilization would probably fragment into lots of relatively independent pieces due to slow communication.
