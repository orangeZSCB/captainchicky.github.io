It seems Springer approves of the general idea of the RWA, but hasn’t explicitly endorsed it. There’s a letter from them here.
http://poynder.blogspot.com/2012/01/springer-statement-on-us-research-works.html

Specifically, they oppose rules requiring open access, unless they get extra cash—they prefer to say they oppose “unfunded mandates”.

Even if we do end up moving to a model of paying publishers exorbitant fees for publishing, the ‘author-pays’ models of open access at least seem to get us out of the current hostage situation.
