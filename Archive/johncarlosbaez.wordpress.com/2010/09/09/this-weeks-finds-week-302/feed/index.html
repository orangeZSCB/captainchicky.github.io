]]> By: John F https://johncarlosbaez.wordpress.com/2010/09/09/this-weeks-finds-week-302/#comment-1414 Tue, 14 Sep 2010 22:23:40 +0000 http://johncarlosbaez.wordpress.com/?p=919#comment-1414 Speaking of correctly modeling forcing, an interesting new paper ” Long-term Evolution of Sunspot Magnetic Fields”
http://arxiv.org/abs/1009.0784v1
argues that long term and recent trends in observed infrared spectra and magnetic field strengths imply that a tipping point in sunspot activity is currently being reached, right now as it were. If that is correct, and if it is correct that sunspot activity is correlated with solar irradiance (e.g. the Maunder Minimum), then we may be at the start of another Capital Minimum.

One takeaway would be that efforts are misplaced that depend upon making sure that the recent solar cycle normal min-max change of > 1 W/m2 is modeled to 5 more decimal points.
