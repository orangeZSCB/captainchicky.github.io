In general, the QED cross section (photon interaction probability in this case) scales as 1/s, where s is the center of mass energy. Thus, in the lab frame it scales as 1/E , E being the photon energy.
On the contrary, the neutrino interaction cross section is proportional to E until ~50 TeV (E being the neutrino energy).
and then goes logaritmically.
The intuition we have from ”everyday life” comes from QED, therefore it’s hard to explain neutrino behaviour.
