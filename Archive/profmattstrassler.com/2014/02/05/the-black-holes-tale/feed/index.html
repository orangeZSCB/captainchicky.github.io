]]> By: duffieldjohn https://profmattstrassler.com/2014/02/05/the-black-holes-tale/#comment-174522 Fri, 07 Feb 2014 09:44:06 +0000 http://profmattstrassler.com/?p=7211#comment-174522 Matt: the poem is nice enough, but IMHO you need to address the physics here. The waterfall analogy is not supported by GR. There is no sense in which space falls inwards in a gravitational field – this Chicken Little myth persists because it’s promoted by people like Max Tegmark. So why doesn’t the light get out of a black hole? You’re standing with your vertical laser pointer on a gedanken planet. We make it smaller and denser, but at no point does the light beam bend round or slow down or fall back. You know about gravitational time dilation and the coordinate speed of light. You know the coordinate speed of light is said to be zero at the event horizon. You know that virtual particles aren’t actual particles that “pop” into existence. You know that there are no negative-energy particles. And you know that the coordinate speed of light applies to Hawking radiation too. IMHO when you do address the physics, you come to appreciate that this whole thing is hypothetical hustle, hyped into a “woo, paradox” soundbite that can be milked.
