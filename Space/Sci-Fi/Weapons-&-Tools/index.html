Ion propulsion - the propulsion of highly energetic particles, usually in a beam. Usually the propulsed particle is an atom in which propulsion is acheived through magnetic fields or something similar. Since charged particles rapidly diverge due to the occurance of similar electric charges, right before exit, adding additional electrons to the propulsed particle or of the like would create a better beam, since neutral particles will not diverge as fast as charged ones. Damages target through the delivery of excessive energy.

There are a few designs, and a prominant one is a modified ion engine. By funnelling charged particles into a concentrated beam, instead of letting them diverge quickly, an ion beam could be made.

However, there is a problem. This beam is weak. Super weak. The built in efficienty of the engine results in the force it produces to be quivalent to pushing a peice of paper half a meter or so, and by no means is the concentrated beam going to do any better. Instead, we can attempt to disregard efficiency, and instead just heat up the input until some portion of it is plasma and beam it out. A shallower and less strong beam that doesn't travel far enough to be used as a ray type weapon can be used as a torch or lightsaber.

Chemical reactions - an uncontrolled or controlled release of energy created via chemical reactions, usually employing a fuel and an oxidizer, though not always.

It can be used to create concentrated beam of energetic particles with the energy, which can be employed like an ion propulsion weapon or an engine. As an engine, it can be used to power missiles, and the structure of such is shown below.


Furthermore, they can be used as a warhead on a missile. The warhead consists of a detonator which helps set off the explosion, and explosive fuel which will carry out the detonation once the reaction starts from the detonator trigger. The detonator creates a trigger, such as a pulse of energy or a spark that can induce the explsive inside the detonator itself go go odd, which sets off the main charge. There are many different kinds of explosive fuel, and high explosives, such as organic peroxides, plastic explosives or nitroglycerin are the most efficient. This is because they generate a detonation without a needed confinement compared to low explosives such as gunpowder, and some, such as organic peroxides have their own oxygen fuel with them, meaning an oxidizer isn't needed.

A more enhanced type of chemical explosive weapon called volumetric weapons requires an oxygen atmosphere to employ, which makes it impractical in interstellar space or on celestial bodies without an oxygen atmosphere. However, in celestial bodies with oxygen, the produce a super powerful detonation. Inside a volumetric weapon, there is a primary detonator, which will explode and propel the fuel as a fine mist and secondary detonators into the air. Afterwards, the fuel diffused out as a mist and in contact with oxygen, will rapidly detonate, creating an extremely massive series of shockwave and a strong updraft due to the partial vacuum resulting from the consuption of oxygen. If the fuel doesn't go off due to the primary explosive, the secondary explosives that were propelled by the initial blast will detonate as well, making sure the fuel in the oxygen rich air will go off.

The ability for the fuel to disperse and the resulting oxygen depleted air means it can easily mutilate, incapacitate, and kill biological targets within crevices.

EMPs - a pulse of electromagnetic radiation, created by objects that can generate large amounts of electromagnetic energy, usually in a beam or shockwave used to short-wire electronics. In a nutshell, an EMP is the sudden transfer of a large amount of charge from one place to another.

A mechanism that can be used to create an EMP is such as follows. A metal cylinder denoated as an armature, is surrounded by a coil of wire. The armature cylinder is filled with a high explosive, and a sturdy jacket surrounds the entire device. The stator winding and the armature cylinder are separated by empty space. There is a power source, such as a bank of capacitors, which would be be connected to the stator. During deployment, a switch connects the capacitors to the stator, sending an electrical current through the wires. This generates an intense magnetic field. Then, a fuse mechanism ignites the explosive material. The explosion travels as a wave through the middle of the armature cylinder. As the explosion makes its way through the cylinder, the cylinder comes in contact with the stator winding. This creates a short circuit, cutting the stator off from its power supply. The moving short circuit compresses the magnetic field, generating the intense electromagnetic burst.

Such a method generates low frequency electrical waves, which may have some limitations.

A second method involves accelerating a high current electron beam against a mesh anode. Many electrons will pass through the anode, forming a bubble of space charge behind the anode. Under the proper conditions, this space charge region will oscillate at microwave frequencies. If the space charge region is placed into a resonant cavity which is appropriately tuned, very high peak powers may be achieved. Conventional microwave engineering techniques may then be used to extract microwave power from the resonant cavity. Because the frequency of oscillation is dependent upon the electron beam parameters, such a method may be tuned or chirped in frequency, where the microwave cavity will support appropriate modes.

A third type of EMP is generated via a nuclear explosion. This however requires that the celestial body in which detonation of the nuclear explosion host an ionosphere. Thus, any celestial body that does not have an atmosphere or in interstellar space, such a method will not generate an EMP.

A nuclear weapon when detonate will release a lot of thermal energy, which means that the resulting upward and outward column of hot air moves the ionosphere overhead. The moving charge of the ionosphere is what causes the bulk of the EMP. A nuclear detonation may also ionize the surrounding air and then move that charge, which may contribute to the EMP as well. When detonated above the ionosphere, more charge can be moved, creating a bigger EMP.

Railguns - a weapon that uses magnetic fields to accelerate objects, ranging from relatively small to arbitrarily large in size whom are able to interact with a magnetic field either due to compatable charge or spin, to a high speed to incapacitate or destroy an entity. It is to be noted that a projectile may not be directly fired by the magnetic field, and instead the projectile is accelerated by an object that is itself accelerated by the magnetic field instead.

The projectile can be designed to penetrate an armored target, and may carry and EMP, biological or chemical agents, and of the like. Or, the projectile may be an explosive, and in this case it is comparable to a missile, although lacking an engine to propel itself.

Magnetic fields - a magnetic field which may be extremely powerful, such as one around a trillion tesla or of the like, which can be used to redirect particles capable of interacting with it. Magnetic fields in practical use are typically generated by electric currents running through conductive material, called electromagnets. Electromagnetic strength can be increased through a variety of factors. By the law of induction,

we can increase the amount of loops to increase strength. Furthermore, by the magnetic field equation inside a solenoid, we can increase the thickness of the wrapping wire and increase the strength of the electricity running through the wire to increase magnetic field strength.

And lastly, by decreasing the temperature to lower wire resistance, strength of the magnetic field can be increased. To lower the temperature, laser cooling of the wire can be implemented, and the use of evaporative cooling can work to some extent as well, resulting in a wire barely above the temperature threshold of Bose-Einstein condensate formation.

Due to a magnetic field able to redirect all charged particles and even neutral particles to some extend, dependent on their intrinsic spin, a "force field" can be created this way.

It is to be noted that the ability to create more copies of itself via resources around it may sometimes be unfeasible due to lack of correct resources. It can be assumed that grey goo nanomachines would contain metals in them, but metals are usualy rare compared to others such as carbon, oxygen, hydrogen, or of the like making replication hard in some environments.

Another thing that must be considered is energy. A grey goo nanomachine must be made such that there is a net energy gain after converting material into more copies of itself.

To create more of itself, grey goo nanomachines have to spend energy to break bonds and reorganize atoms into intermediary molecules. These intermediary molecules have high energy, and therefore are unstable as a large amount of energy is needed to stabilize them. Thus as a result, they will release energy when they are remade and finally collapse into the lower energy state, which is the final product of more grey goo nanomachines. Notice the fact that to get to the tipping point of energy release, there must be an initial input of energy. Depending on how the nanomachine is contructed, this initial input of energy may be very high, which means that the conversion of matter into more grey goo nanomachines by the nanomachines will be slow in an inversely proportional relationship.

Neutron-pasta based weapon - by taking out and keeping neutron pasta compressed from a neutron star through the use of very strong magnetic fields, or graviton manipulation; or by compressing neutrons via magnetic fields or graviton manipulation to create neutron pasta. It is to be noted that the neutron pasta inside neutron stars is buried under a very strong crust, making it very hard to get. Creating neutron pasta may be the more viable option.

Although neutron pasta is made out of almost all neutrons which have neutral charge, due to their particle spin, a magnetic field is still able to hold them together. By incorporating a super strength magnetic field, the sufficient force from electromagnetism should be enough to emulate the intense gravity of neutron stars.

One can then transport said neutron pasta, to an area and decompress it, returning the neutron pasta to normal density, which will release a lot of energy whilst doing so. In sufficient quantities, the resulting explosion may be compared to a small supernovae.

Strong and weak interaction

Fusion and fission based nuclear reactions - a controlled or uncontrolled release of energy created by the fusion or fission of atomic nuclei, typically uranium-237 or plutonium-241 for fission based reactions and deuterium or hydrogen for fusion based reactions. Usually in the form of an explosion or detonation as a missile or a warhead on a missile. Detonation also generates EMP shockwave.

Strangelets - a negatively charged droplet of up, down, and strange quarks in roughly equal ratios, capable of being a seeding vector for converting normal matter into strange matter. The dissolution of particles, notably neutrons into individual quarks in quark-gluon plasma lowers its energy state. If the Bodmer-Witten hypothesis holds true, such a state may be more stable than normal matter.



The production of strange quarks shown above results in the formation of strange matter. If the negative charge of strange and down quarks dominates more than the positive charge of up quarks, then negatively charged strange matter will not be repelled by positively charged normal matter as per the Coulomb repulsion force and convert any atomic nuclei it is able to interact with into more strange matter. If the strangelet is positively charged, it may still be able to interact with atomic nuclei by overcoming Coulomb repulsion through kinetic energy though further interactions will not get far, making positively charged strangelets practically useless as a weapon.

Matter-antimatter annihilation - by keeping matter and antimatter suspended via a magnetic field or graviton manipulation, one can transport such to a target place and cause annihilation, converting the matter and antimatter into energy which may cause damage.


Gravity

Induced black hole - sudden compression of a particle or group of particles such that escape velocity from the region in which it is concentrated exceeds the speed of light. The minimum required energy for a creation of such in 4 dimensional spacetime is 1016 TeV, and can be acheived by suddenly compressing charged particles or colliding them at high speeds and confining them to a small space such that an event horizon is formed. Graviton manipulation could also be used to create the black hole. Since the particles creating the black hole has charge, it can be propelled via magnetic fields as a form of "gun", or left at its materialization spot to cause destruction via the absorption of surrounding matter. Also note that such black holes if not able to sustain themselves will evaporate via Hawking Radiation so induced black holes that evaporate too quickly to be used as a matter absorption weapon can be also used as a bomb.


Large-scale destruction

Black hole bomb - by building a large structure of encompassing mirrors, one can harvest and hold energy from a spinning black hole's ergosphere via the Penrose process and superradiant scattering in which concentrating it can create an uncontrolled explosion. Depending on mirror integrity, it can acheive an explosion comparable to a supernova.

Vacuum decay - if the Higgs field is metastable, through an active trigger such as a very energetic explosion/collision and induced quantum tunneling, or through a passive trigger such as a black hole, the false vacuum can decay into a true vacuum, expanding in a sphere through spacetime that will destroy present configurations of matter when the bubble's walls reach it, and inside the true vacuum, physics may be completely different than usual. The expanding bubble of true vacuum has positive surface tension, since energy is expended as the fields roll over the potential barrier to the true vacuum. The former ir proportional to the cube of the bubble's radius while the latter is proportional to the square of its radius, so there is a critical size in which the bubble can grow; as a result bubbles below the critical size will shrink, vice versa. This means bubbles with a radius below critical size could be used for wiping out smaller areas of spacetime without endangering the universe itself. Small bubbles of true vacuum can also be inflated to the critical size by providing energy and expand towards infinity with its speed approaching the speed of light extremely quickly if there is a large difference in energy between the false and true vacuums. The bubble itself also does not produce any gravitational effects because the negative energy density of the bubble interior is cancelled out by the positive kinetic energy of the wall.

Supernova catapult - by changing the velocity of a dying star and directing it towards a target via stellar engines, the star may undergo supernova near or at the target and destroy the target as a result.

Biological agents - a usually biological entity like a bacterium, virus, or prion that is able to harm or destroy other usually cell based biological entities.

Propulsion weapons - a weapon that uses a variety of methods such as chemical, electric, or air pressure to accelerate an object at an entity with the intent to incapacitate it, not dissimlar to a railgun.

Time related weapons - a weapon that can somehow manipulate or change time passage in a region of spacetime. Maybe be manipulating such as compressing or stretch a given region of spacetime, or perhaps accelerating a given region of spacetime relative to something else.

Dimensional collapse weapons - a weapon that can collapse the dimension of spacetime into a lower one, typically occurring in a sphere like structure at the speed of light. A civilization may be able to survive via computer emulation of higher dimensions. For example, the Rule 110 cellular automaton is Turing complete, which means that given enough time and space it can do anything that a normal computer can do down to 1 dimension, and by using such, higher dimensions can be simulated in the lower dimension. As for 0 dimensions, possibly something to do with a singularity may work since a singularity has infinite density within a single point in space. There are no negative dimensions.

Black domain - a region of spacetime where the speed of light, and thus the maximum speed of a massive accelerating object is lowered to hamper travel. There is no current mechanism to lower c that I can think of. I originally though by inducing photon mass through the Higgs Field or by creating a scalar field that induces photon mass, c may be lowered, but it appears that the speed a photon travels does not affect c. If c were to be decreased in some manner, it would impact both electromagnetic constants in Maxwell's equations by some factor n<1, making electric fields n times stronger and magnetic fields 1/n times as strong. The fine structure constant Î± would increase, therefore making larger structures such as large atomic nuclei or larger molecules unstable are Î± gets larger. Reducing c also causes nuclear energy production to decrease as per e=mc2, making stars shrink as they reach a new equilibrium with gravity.

Disabling the electromagnetic field - the ability to disable the electromagnetic field in a given region of spacetime, resulting in the dissolution of molecules due to electrons being unable to interact and flying away from their parent nuclei, and the collapse of atomic nuclei due to the strong force taking over.

Disabling the strong nuclear field - the ability to disable the strong nuclear field in a given region of spacetime resulting in the dissolution of atoms due to the resulting unconfined quarks.

Disabling the Higgs field - the ability to disable the Higgs field in a given region of spacetime resulting in particles becoming massless, gaining momentum and flying off at c before regaining mass and losing momentum when it flies out of the area of effect.

Causing radioactive nuclei to decay - the manipulation of the weak and strong nuclear force and occasionally the electromagnetic force to induce decay of all unstable nuclei within a certain region of spacetime causing massive energy release.

Graviton manipulation - the ability to manipulate spacetime and the subsequent gravitational field to induce greater attraction of an object to a specific area.

Artificially induced supernova - by either removing fusion from a star's core(i.e. strong interaction manipulation), or by creating enough radiation pressure that gravity cannot hold a star together, one of the most violent explosions in the universe can be induced.

Wormholes - by creating negative mass matter, one can prop open wormholes(which will collapse into singularities if left un-sustained) and travel great distances.

The Alcubierre drive - a method in which a massive object could achieve apparent faster-than-light travel if a configurable energy-density field lower than that of the vacuum could be created via negative mass matter. In other words a massive object would traverse distances by contracting space in front of it and expanding space behind it to achieve better travel speeds. It is to be noted that were an Alcubierre-driven ship to decelerate from superluminal speed, the particles that its bubble had gathered in transit would be released in energetic outbursts akin to the infinitely-blueshifted radiation hypothesized to occur at the inner event horizon of a Kerr black hole; forward-facing particles would thereby be energetic enough to destroy anything at the destination directly in front of the ship, so it can also be used as a weapon.
